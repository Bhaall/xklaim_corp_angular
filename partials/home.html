how xKlaim works

Real-time Offers. Real-world Results.

It takes just seconds to create and activate an offer with xKlaim. The best part? Your customers can actually use xKlaim to ask for an offer—which you can respond to in real time.

Social Activation, Revenue Generation.

Fulfill the potential built up by your brand's social media presence. xKlaim gives you the most effective way to target followers with a desirable offer, sent at an ideal time—even to direct them to specific locations.

Mobile Reach, Delivery and Fulfillment.

xKlaim lets you reach customers where they are right now: out in the world, on their mobile devices. With offers generated on-screen in the form of virtual cards, xKlaim reduces the fall-off between intention and redemption.

Industry-standard Security. And Beyond.

xKlaim complies with Level 1 Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards. But even beyond PCI compliance, xKlaim is redeemable only at stores you specify. Every xKlaim virtual card is time-sensitive, so unused cards expire and your money is automatically returned.

No Extra Fees. No, Really.

xKlaim works just like any regular credit or debit card. When a shopper presents an xKlaim virtual card, all the cashier has to do is scan the barcode image or enter the card number. Thats it—no extra fees are charged and no software installations are needed to redeem.

POS-Ready, Set, Go.

From next-generation barcode scanning systems, to legacy registers, xKlaim works with virtually any point-of-sale system that accepts debit and credit cards. There's no new hardware to buy, no new software to install or integrate.
